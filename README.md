## Instructions
Use arrow keys or WASD to move.

Click on objects to pick them up. Tools are automatically put into the hotbar, which is on the bottom of the screen, for use. In order to use these tools, press F.
- Fishing rod: Press F once to cast the rod. A few seconds later, a pop-up appears. Click on all five icons within five seconds in order to successfully catch materials. If you want to cancel a cast, simply press F again. Note that once the pop-up appears, you cannot cancel the cast. Fishing can either drop Wood or Metal.
- Sword: Press F to swing. Use this sword to kill bats for Brains.

Press 1 in order to select the tool in the first slot of the hotbar. Press 2 in order to select the tool in the second slot of the hotbar.

Click on the broken spaceship to reveal the quantity of materials required to repair the spaceship and escape the black hole.

To play the game, go to this link: https://bosshi2.itch.io/the-lost-astronaut
- You must play in full screen. Does not support mobile
